# Model Explainability

## Features Increasing Churn

- MonthlyCharges
- InternetService_Fiber optic
- PaymentMethod_Electronic check
- PaperlessBilling
- SeniorCitizen

Interpretation:

Customers with higher monthly spending and electronic payment methods show higher churn probability.

---

## Features Reducing Churn

- tenure
- Contract_Two year
- Contract_One year
- InternetService_No
- OnlineSecurity_Yes
- TechSupport_Yes
- Dependents
- TotalCharges

Interpretation:

Long-term customers and customers using support/security services are less likely to churn.

---

## Business Interpretation

Customer retention should focus on:

- Long-term contracts
- Customer support
- Security services
- Reducing payment friction

---
